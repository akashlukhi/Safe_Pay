About Me

Safe Pay (stylized as S Pay; formerly Pay with Safe and Android Pay) is a digital wallet

platform and online payment system developed by Safe to power in-app and tap-to-pay

purchases on mobile devices, enabling users to make payments with Android phones, tablets

or computers. In addition to this, the service also supports passes, Bill pays, event tickets,

movie tickets, public transportation tickets, store cards, and mobile Recharge.
